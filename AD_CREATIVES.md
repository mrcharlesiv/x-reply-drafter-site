## 🎨 VISUAL DESIGN SPECS

**All Images:**
- Format: PNG or JPG
- Size: 1200x628px (X recommended)
- Color palette: Dark (#0a0a0f background), bright text (white/cyan)
- Font: Inter or system sans-serif
- Text hierarchy: Large headline (36px+), subtext (18px+)
- No AI-generated imagery (screenshots or stock only)

**Design approach:**
- Minimalist (not cluttered)
- High contrast (readable on mobile)
- One clear focal point
- Single CTA per ad
