# Hero Headline Options - Reply Guy Positioning

## Current (Old)
"Draft viral replies on X in one click"

---

## NEW OPTIONS (Test These)

### Option 1: Direct Threat/Differentiation (STRONGEST)
**Headline:**
"Scale your replies. X can't detect it."

**Subheadline:**
"Reply bots get shadowbanned. ViralDraft doesn't—because YOU click. 50+ replies/day without detection."

**Why it works:** Leads with the actual competitive advantage. Speaks to fear (detection) and solution (undetectable). Immediately differentiates from bots.

---

### Option 2: Strategy-First (AUTHORITATIVE)
**Headline:**
"The reply guy strategy. At scale."

**Subheadline:**
"50-100 replies/day is the fastest way to grow on X. X shadowbans bots. We bypass detection because you're the one clicking."

**Why it works:** Positions you as understanding THE growth strategy. Speaks to people who already know this works. Explains the difference from bots clearly.

---

### Option 3: Fear + Safe (EMOTIONAL)
**Headline:**
"Your competitors are using reply bots. They'll get banned."

**Subheadline:**
"ViralDraft scales your replies without detection—because you click manually. The only safe way to do this."

**Why it works:** FUD angle is strong. Makes it about competition and risk. Positions ViralDraft as the only safe option.

---

### Option 4: Speed + Safety (BENEFIT-FOCUSED)
**Headline:**
"Reply 50 times/day. Undetected."

**Subheadline:**
"AI assists. You click. X sees a human. No shadowban risk."

**Why it works:** Very direct and specific. Leads with outcome (50 replies) + safety. Short and punchy.

---

### Option 5: Contrarian/Clever (MEMORABLE)
**Headline:**
"Everyone knows: Reply guy strategy = growth. What they don't know: How to scale it without getting caught."

**Subheadline:**
"ViralDraft. Manual interaction, AI speed, undetectable."

**Why it works:** Assumes the reader knows the strategy works. Positions you as solving the real problem (getting caught). More sophisticated/clever positioning.

## COPY TONE

**DO:**
- Direct, specific, confident
- Speak to fear (shadowban) + solution (undetectable)
- Emphasize "YOU click" (manual = safe)
- Concrete numbers (50 replies, 10K+ users, 0 bans)

**DON'T:**
- Sound like a generic AI tool
- Be vague about how it works
- Oversell speed if it means losing the "you click" message
- Use corporate language

## WHY THESE BEAT THE OLD COPY

**Old:** "Draft viral replies on X in one click"
- Generic, could describe any AI reply tool
- Doesn't differentiate
- Doesn't lead with pain point or solution
- No mention of the actual advantage

**New (Option 1):** "Scale your replies. X can't detect it."
- Specific to reply guy growth strategy
- Leads with key differentiation (undetectable)
- Speaks to actual pain (detection/shadowban)
- Creates urgency (competitors are using bots, will get banned)
- Positions as only safe solution

**Conversion uplift estimate:** 20-40% higher CTR (more specific/relevant) + 10-20% higher landing page conversion (better qualified audience)
