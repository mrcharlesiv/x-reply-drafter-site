## 📈 METRICS & KPIs TO TRACK

### Awareness Campaign
- **CPM:** Cost per 1,000 impressions (Target: $2-$5)
- **CTR:** Click-through rate (Target: 2-4%)
- **Reach:** Unique users who see ad
- **Frequency:** Average times shown per user (Target: 2-3)

### Conversion Campaign
- **CPC:** Cost per click (Target: $0.75-$1.50)
- **CTR:** Click-through rate (Target: 1.5-3%)
- **Conversion Rate:** Clicks → signups (Target: 5-10%)
- **Cost per signup:** (Target: $10-$25)
- **Cost per install:** (Target: $8-$20)

### Overall
- **ROAS:** Return on ad spend (Target: 3:1 or better)
- **LTV:** Lifetime value per user
- **CAC:** Customer acquisition cost
- **Payback Period:** Months to recover ad spend
- **Retention:** % still using after 30 days
